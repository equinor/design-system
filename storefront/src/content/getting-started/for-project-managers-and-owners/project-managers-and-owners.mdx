# For project managers and project owners
 
The Equinor design system (EDS) will help your projects be more consistent and reach production faster. 
The EDS will be the prefered UI language (design) and components (development) for all of Equinor's internal digital interfaces. 

We hope that you will allow and encourage your team to help contribute to the system once it is released.

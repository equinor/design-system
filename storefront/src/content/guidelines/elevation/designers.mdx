## Elevation in Figma

* Select the element you want to apply elevation to.  
* In the sidebar under Effects, pick the 'Styles' menu. This is the button with the 4 dots
  * :warning: Image example like: https://i.imgur.com/jH1JSog.png
* The styles menu will open adn you can choose the elevation you need.
  * :warning: Image example like: https://i.imgur.com/eYPY1Up.png

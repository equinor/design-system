# Inviting members [​](#inviting-members)

### Step 1: Under your project name, you'll find a small menu. [​](#step-1-under-your-project-name-youll-find-a-small-menu.)

![](/images/InviteMembers01.png)

### Step 2: Click on 'Invite members. [​](#step-2-click-on-invite-members.)

This will open a page.

![](/images/InviteMembers02.png)

### Step 3: Here you see all the current members. [​](#step-3-here-you-see-all-the-current-members.)

Via the button 'Invite users' you will be able to invite new members

![](/images/InviteMembers03.png)

### Step 4: Just drop the mail address and hit the button! [​](#step-4-just-drop-the-mail-address-and-hit-the-button)

An invite will be sent to the email address.

![](/images/InviteMembers04a.png)

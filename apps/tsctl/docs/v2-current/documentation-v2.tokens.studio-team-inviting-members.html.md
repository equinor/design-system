# Inviting Members [​](#inviting-members)

Invite your team to collaborate on design tokens in Studio.

## Inviting to a Workspace [​](#inviting-to-a-workspace)

1.  Go to **Workspace Members** in the sidebar
    
2.  Click **Invite Member**
    
    ![Workspace members](/images/team/org-members-light.png)![Workspace members](/images/team/org-members-dark.png)
    
3.  Enter the person's email address
    
4.  Select a workspace role (Admin or Member)
    
    ![Add member dialog](/images/team/add-member-dialog-light.png)![Add member dialog](/images/team/add-member-dialog-dark.png)
    
5.  Click **Send Invitation**
    

The person receives an email with a link to join. If they don't have a Studio account, they'll be prompted to create one.

## Inviting to a Project [​](#inviting-to-a-project)

After someone is a workspace member, you can add them to specific projects:

1.  Go to **Members** under SETTINGS in the project sidebar
    
2.  Click **Add Member**
    
3.  Select the member from your workspace
    
4.  Assign a project role (Owner, Admin, Editor, or Viewer)
    
5.  Click **Add**
    
    ![Project members](/images/settings/project-members-light.png)![Project members](/images/settings/project-members-dark.png)
    

## Pending Invitations [​](#pending-invitations)

You can see and manage pending invitations:

1.  Go to **Workspace Members** in the sidebar
2.  Switch to the **Pending** tab
3.  Resend or cancel invitations as needed

Studio sends reminder emails for pending invitations automatically.

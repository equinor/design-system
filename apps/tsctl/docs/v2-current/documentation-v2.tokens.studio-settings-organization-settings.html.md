# Workspace Settings [​](#workspace-settings)

Manage your workspace's configuration, members, and preferences.

## General Settings [​](#general-settings)

Navigate to **Workspace Settings** in the sidebar to manage your workspace profile and preferences.

| Setting | Description |
| --- | --- |
| **Name** | Your workspace's display name |
| **Description** | A description of what this workspace is for |
| **Website** | Your workspace's website URL |
| **Country** | Your workspace's country |
| **Billing Email** | Email address for invoices and billing notifications |
| **Default Base Permission** | Controls implicit access for workspace members on new projects. Options: **Invite only**, **Read**, or **Write** (defaults to Write). Individual projects can override this. See [Roles and Permissions](./../team/roles-and-permissions.html#base-permissions) for details. |

## Members [​](#members)

Manage who has access to your workspace:

-   View all current members and their roles
-   Invite new members by email
-   Change member roles
-   Remove members
-   View and manage pending invitations

![Workspace members](/images/settings/workspace-members-light.png)![Workspace members](/images/settings/workspace-members-dark.png)

See [Inviting Members](./../team/inviting-members.html) and [Roles and Permissions](./../team/roles-and-permissions.html) for details.

## Activity [​](#activity)

The **Activity** page provides an audit log of governance and operational activity for your workspace. You can filter by category and project to track changes such as member invitations, role assignments, and settings updates.

![Workspace activity log](/images/settings/workspace-activity-light.png)![Workspace activity log](/images/settings/workspace-activity-dark.png)

## Billing [​](#billing)

Manage your subscription and payment:

-   View your current plan and usage
-   Upgrade or downgrade your plan
-   Manage payment methods
-   View invoices
-   Adjust seat count
-   Change billing interval (monthly/annual)

![Workspace subscription](/images/settings/workspace-subscription-light.png)![Workspace subscription](/images/settings/workspace-subscription-dark.png)

See [Billing and Plans](./billing-and-plans.html) for details.

## Delete Workspace [​](#delete-workspace)

At the bottom of the Workspace Settings page, the **Delete Workspace** section contains irreversible actions such as deleting the workspace. Proceed with caution — once a workspace is deleted, there is no going back.

![Workspace settings danger zone](/images/settings/workspace-settings-danger-zone-light.png)![Workspace settings danger zone](/images/settings/workspace-settings-danger-zone-dark.png)

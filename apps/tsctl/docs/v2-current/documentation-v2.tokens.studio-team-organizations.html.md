# Workspaces [​](#workspaces)

Workspaces are the top-level container in Tokens Studio. They hold your projects, team members, subscription, and settings.

## Workspace Structure [​](#workspace-structure)

![Workspace structure: Settings, Members, Projects, Subscription, and Activity](/images/diagrams/org-structure-light.svg)![Workspace structure: Settings, Members, Projects, Subscription, and Activity](/images/diagrams/org-structure-dark.svg)

## Workspace Sidebar [​](#workspace-sidebar)

The workspace sidebar provides access to:

-   **Settings** — Workspace name, description, website, country, billing email, and delete workspace
-   **Members** — Invite and manage team members with roles
-   **Projects** — Create and manage design token projects
-   **Subscription** — Manage your plan, seats, billing, and payment interval
-   **Activity** — Audit log of workspace events

## Workspace Management Page [​](#workspace-management-page)

Studio now has a dedicated **Workspaces** page where you can see all your workspaces, switch between them, and create new ones. Access it from the workspace switcher in the sidebar.

## Multiple Workspaces [​](#multiple-workspaces)

You can be a member of multiple workspaces. Use the workspace picker in the sidebar to switch between them.

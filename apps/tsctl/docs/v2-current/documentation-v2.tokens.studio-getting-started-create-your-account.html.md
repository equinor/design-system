# Create Your Account [​](#create-your-account)

Get started with Studio by creating your account and setting up your first workspace.

## Sign Up [​](#sign-up)

You can create a Studio account in two ways:

### Email and Password [​](#email-and-password)

1.  Go to the Studio sign-up page
2.  Enter your email address and choose a password
3.  Check your email for a confirmation link
4.  Click the link to verify your account

### Sign in with Google [​](#sign-in-with-google)

1.  Go to the Studio sign-up page
2.  Click **Sign in with Google**
3.  Select your Google account
4.  Your account is created automatically — no email verification needed

TIP

If you sign up with Google, you can add a password later from your account settings. This lets you use either method to sign in.

## Create a Workspace [​](#create-a-workspace)

After signing up for the first time, Studio guides you through creating your first workspace as part of the onboarding flow. Workspaces are the top-level container for everything in Studio — they hold your projects, team members, and billing.

During onboarding, you'll:

1.  Enter a name for your workspace (e.g., your company name)
2.  Choose a URL slug — this creates your workspace's unique URL
3.  Add optional details like description, website, and country

Studio creates your workspace automatically once you complete these steps.

INFO

You can be a member of multiple workspaces. Switch between them from the workspace picker in the sidebar.

## Invite Your Team [​](#invite-your-team)

Once your workspace is set up, invite your team:

1.  Go to **Workspace Members** in the sidebar
    
2.  Click **Invite Member**
    
    ![Workspace members page](/images/getting-started/org-members-light.png)![Workspace members page](/images/getting-started/org-members-dark.png)
    
3.  Enter their email address
    
4.  Choose a role:
    
    -   **Admin** — Full access to workspace settings, billing, and all projects
    -   **Member** — Access to projects they're added to
5.  Click **Send Invitation**
    

The invited person will receive an email with a link to join your workspace.

## Choose a Plan [​](#choose-a-plan)

Studio offers several plans to fit your team's needs. You can start with a free trial and upgrade when you're ready.

To manage your plan:

1.  Go to **Subscription** in the workspace sidebar
    
2.  Click **Change Plan** to review available plans
    
    ![Subscription page](/images/settings/workspace-subscription-light.png)![Subscription page](/images/settings/workspace-subscription-dark.png)
    
3.  Select your preferred plan and complete the checkout process

# Billing and Plans [​](#billing-and-plans)

Studio offers several plans to fit your team's needs, managed through Stripe. All plans include a 14-day free trial with no credit card required.

## Available Plans [​](#available-plans)

### Variables [​](#variables)

For teams managing Figma variables at scale.

-   **Companion app** + **Studio platform** access
-   1 editor seat included, unlimited additional
-   Unlimited viewer seats
-   Up to 5 variable-based projects
-   Variables & Figma sync via Companion
-   Exports & Releases

INFO

The Variables plan is focused on Figma Variables as the source of truth. All projects on this plan are automatically set to variable-based — you cannot create token-based projects. See [Project Types](./../getting-started/project-types.html) for details on what's included in variable-based projects.

### Starter Plus [​](#starter-plus)

Our plugin for pros who need more control inside Figma.

-   **Figma plugin** access only (no Studio platform)
-   1 editor seat included, unlimited additional seats can be purchased
-   Direct support in Slack
-   Advanced token types
-   Multi-file sync via your own Git
-   Your own git with branching
-   Token documentation generation

### Essential [​](#essential)

Full access to the Studio platform.

-   **Figma plugin** + **Companion app** + **Studio platform** access
-   1 editor seat included, up to 3 additional seats can be purchased
-   Unlimited viewer seats
-   1 project included
-   Direct support in Slack
-   Figma plugin included
-   Versioning & releases
-   Direct-to-code workflow

### Organization [​](#organization)

Scale your design system with the full Studio platform.

-   **Figma plugin** + **Companion app** + **Studio platform** access
-   5 editor seats included, unlimited additional seats can be purchased
-   Unlimited viewer seats
-   Unlimited projects
-   Onboarding & Priority support
-   Everything from Essential
-   Branching

Pricing is shown on the subscription page within Studio and varies by billing interval (monthly or annual). Annual billing saves up to 20%.

## Subscription Overview [​](#subscription-overview)

Navigate to **Workspace Subscription** in the sidebar to view your current plan details, including your plan tier, billing amount, seat count, next billing date, and billing cycle.

![Subscription status and billing overview](/images/settings/workspace-subscription-light.png)![Subscription status and billing overview](/images/settings/workspace-subscription-dark.png)

From this page you can manage your subscription using four action buttons:

-   **Change Plan** — Switch between available plan tiers
-   **Manage Seats** — Add or remove seats from your subscription
-   **Manage Billing** — Open the Stripe billing portal to update payment methods and view invoices
-   **Change Interval** — Switch between monthly and annual billing

## Changing Your Plan [​](#changing-your-plan)

Click **Change Plan** to open the plan selection dialog. You can see available plan tiers with their pricing and switch between monthly and annual billing.

![Change subscription plan dialog](/images/settings/billing-change-plan-dialog-light.png)![Change subscription plan dialog](/images/settings/billing-change-plan-dialog-dark.png)

Select a new plan and confirm to upgrade or downgrade. Changes take effect based on your billing cycle.

Downgrading to Variables

Switching to the Variables plan requires that your workspace has no token-based projects, no active branches, and no published documentation. You'll be prompted to resolve these before the downgrade can proceed.

## Managing Seats [​](#managing-seats)

Click **Manage Seats** to adjust the number of seats in your subscription. Your plan includes a base number of editor seats, and you can add more at an additional cost per seat per month. Viewer seats are always unlimited and free on plans that include them.

![Manage additional seats dialog](/images/settings/billing-manage-seats-dialog-light.png)![Manage additional seats dialog](/images/settings/billing-manage-seats-dialog-dark.png)

Enter the number of additional seats you need and click **Review Changes** to see a summary before confirming.

## Changing Billing Interval [​](#changing-billing-interval)

Click **Change Interval** to switch between monthly and annual billing. The dialog shows a price summary comparing your current and new billing amounts.

![Change billing interval dialog](/images/settings/billing-change-interval-dialog-light.png)![Change billing interval dialog](/images/settings/billing-change-interval-dialog-dark.png)

Changes take effect at the end of your current billing period.

## Billing Portal [​](#billing-portal)

Click **Manage Billing** to open the Stripe billing portal, where you can update payment methods, view and download past invoices, and manage your billing details.

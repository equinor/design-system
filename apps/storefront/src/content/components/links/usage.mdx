## Figma

### How to add

1. Select the text in the frame/artboard. 
2. Locate the **Design** tab in the **Inspector Panel**.
3. Under the **Fill** section, apply an `Interactive color` to the text. 
4. Under the **Text** section, open the overflow menu and apply the **Decoration** underline to the text.

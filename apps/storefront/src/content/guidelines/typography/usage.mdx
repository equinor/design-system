## Figma


### How to add

1. Locate the **layer** in the **Layers Panel** that needs typography style applied.
2. Locate the [Design tab :warning:]() in the **Inspector Panel**.
3. Under the **Text** section, open the [Style library :warning:]() menu to view the text styles in order of relevance.
  * The styles in the sections `Headings`and `Paragraph`are the most relevant. 
4. Hover styles to see their names and supplemental information. Choose a style by clicking on the style needed.

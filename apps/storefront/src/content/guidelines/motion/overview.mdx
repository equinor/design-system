Application of motion, animated typography and page transitions can help contribute to a dynamic site experience. Motion should be used subtly and only as an aid to the experience and messaging. 


**Brand Center Toolbox**

Please refer to the Equinor [Brand Center](https://communicationtoolbox.equinor.com/brandcenter/en/equinorbc/component/default/2037 "Brand Center Toolbox Guidelines") for guidelines using motion.

## Figma


### How to add

1. Click `A` or `F`on the keyboard to open the predefined list of frame sizes.
2. Locate the [Design tab :warning:]() in the **Inspector Panel**.
3. Choose a frame preset by clicking on the preset name.  
4. With the artboard selected in the **Layers pannel**, locate the [Design tab :warning:]() in the **Inspector Panel**.
5. Under the **Layout grid** section, open the [Style library :warning:]() menu to view the grid styles.
6. Choose a style by clicking on the style needed. 
  * Only one grid can be applied at a time.

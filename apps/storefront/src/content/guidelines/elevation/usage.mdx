## Figma


### How to add

1. Locate the **layer** in the *Layers Panel* that needs elevation applied.
2. Locate the [Design tab :warning:]() in the *Inspector Panel*.
3. Under the *Effects* section, open the [Style library :warning:]() menu to view the elevation styles.
4. Hover styles to see their names and supplemental information. Choose a style by clicking on the elevation needed.

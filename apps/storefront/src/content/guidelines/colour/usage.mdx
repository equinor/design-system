## Figma


### How to add

1. Locate the **layer** in the *Layers Panel* that needs colour applied.
2. Locate the [Design tab :warning:]() in the *Inspector Panel*.
3. Under the *Fill* section, open the [Style library :warning:]() menu to view the colour styles in order of relevance.
4. Hover styles to see their names and supplemental information. Choose a style by clicking on the color needed.
